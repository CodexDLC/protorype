SONIC STORYTELLING

With over three decades of dedicated experience (since 1993), I specialize in directing Foley sessions and mixing complex cinematic soundscapes. As a Lead Foley Mixer and Sound Supervisor, I oversee the entire Foley production process—from recording to the final mix.

Building on a distinguished career in Ukraine, including a long-standing tenure as Lead Foley Mixer at Foley Walkers, I have contributed to Hollywood features, Netflix originals, and AAA game cinematics. My recent work on the acclaimed feature The Goat Life earned our team a 2025 MPSE Golden Reel Award nomination.

Now based in Germany, I bring 33 years of technical mastery to remote freelance collaborations, Foley supervision, and on-site studio mixing worldwide.
